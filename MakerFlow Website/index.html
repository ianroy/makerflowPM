Why this product exists

Most PM tools optimize for generic software teams. Lab and makerspace teams need scheduling, inventory, shared-space operations, and service-oriented workflows on top of standard project management.

  • Manage spaces and machines alongside projects.
  • Track consumables by location and owner.
  • Coordinate staff, student workers, and service requests.
  • Export everything without vendor lock-in.

Why use this too?

MakerFlow PM is built for organizations where service delivery and operations are inseparable from project execution.

Operational Visibility

Unify project status, room/equipment readiness, and inventory state in one dashboard.

Delegation at Scale

Role-based views for directors, staff, and student workers to reduce coordination load.

Faster Onboarding

Template-based onboarding boards with checklists linked to your actual team workflows.

Portfolio Governance

Cross-space reporting, audit trails, and configurable permissions for multi-department networks.

Differentiated Feature Set

Pitch style inspired by modern PM tools, but tailored to shared lab operations.

  • Kanban + List + Calendar views with direct inline editing and drag-and-drop updates.
  • Workspaces, teams, and spaces for multi-department or multi-site organizations.
  • Space administration for equipment assets and consumables by location.
  • Agenda and onboarding systems designed for recurring meetings and role-based training.
  • Comment threads + @mentions on tasks and projects with optional email notifications.
  • Granular data controls including deleted-item queues, restore/purge workflow, and audit trail.
  • Open-source and self-hostable architecture with no external runtime dependencies.
  • CSV portability + sync tooling for migration safety and long-term ownership.
